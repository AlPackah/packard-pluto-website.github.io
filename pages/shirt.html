This Stellar Shirt Could Be Yours!

  • Step 1. Find a Sticker

    #VivaLaPluto stickers have been scattered across downtown Washington DC. We suggest you start your adventure at the National Mall.

    If you need a hint, ask the folks over at the Air and Space Museum. They're a very reliable bunch.

  • Step 2. Instagram It

    When you find a sticker post a photo of it on Instagram with the hashtag #VivaLaPluto. The more you find the better your chances of winning.

    You have until June 30th to post all and any #VivaLaPluto stickers you've found.

  • Step 3. Tune In

    Be sure to either attend the Exlusive Pluto Livestream, or tune in at home. Neil DeGrasse Tyson will announce the 9 winners at the end of the livestream.
